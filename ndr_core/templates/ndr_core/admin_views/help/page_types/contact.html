Contact Form Page

A Contact Form Page features a form where visitors can send you a message. The contact form itself consists of a subject line, a field for the user's email address and a text field for the message. You have two options how to deal with user messages. You can store them on the server and look at them there or you can forward them to a specified email address.

How To Configure your Contact Form Settings

  • Go to Settings > Mail Settings and set the message forward option to "keep" or "forward".
  • If you choose "keep", the messages will be available in your Inbox.
  • If you choose "forward", you need to specify the mail-server configuration to send the messages to the specified address. The messages are still available over the Inbox but they are archived automatically.
