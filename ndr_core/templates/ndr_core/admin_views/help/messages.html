How Manage Messages

If you want to get in touch with users of your web site, you can provide a contact form (see Contact Form Page) to let them send you messages. By default, these messages are stored on your server and you can look at them in the administration interface. Alternatively, you can forward these messages to a given email address and deal with them there.

How To Configure your Messages Settings

  • Go to Settings > Mail Settings and set the message forward option to "keep" or "forward".
  • If you choose "keep", the messages will be available in your Inbox.
  • If you choose "forward", you need to specify the mail-server configuration to send the messages to the specified address. The messages are still available over the Inbox but they are archived automatically.
